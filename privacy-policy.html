Privacy Policy for ShiftPay

Effective date: 24 June 2026

ShiftPay is a simple shift pay calculator designed to help users calculate estimated earnings from hourly work, breaks, overtime, tips, deductions, and saved shift history.

Information We Collect

ShiftPay does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information with us or with third parties.

Any shift calculations, saved shifts, settings, or history are stored locally on your device only. This information is not sent to any external server by ShiftPay.

How We Use Information

Locally stored information is used only to provide app functionality, such as saving previous shift calculations and user preferences.

Data Sharing

ShiftPay does not share user data with third parties.

Internet Access

ShiftPay is designed to work offline. The app does not require an account, login, or cloud sync.

Children’s Privacy

ShiftPay is not specifically directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Because ShiftPay stores app data locally on your device, you are responsible for managing access to your device and its security settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
